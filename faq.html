FAQ

What is Howl?

Howl is a bot for Discord. He will help you improve your Discord server by organizing and moderating channels, roles and members and by providing entertainment.

The name of this bot refers to the anime film Howl's Moving Castle and Howl is a powerful wizard living in the land of Ingary. The character I chose (My girlfriend chose*) is better known as Howl or Howl Pendragon, but I decided to use the more simple title "Howl".

Why should I use Howl?

Howl is designed to satisfy all your needs for you Discord server. I personally don't like to have a ton of bots on my server, so I decided to create one, that can do almost everything. While doing this, it was very important to me, that each feature is Utilities and high quality as many multi-purpose Discord bots have many but poorly designed features.

How to get started?

First of all, you may invite Howl to your Discord server. After that you should take a look into !help or Commands to see everything Howl can do. If you need a detailed help for a command, you can either type the command without any arguments, e.g. !avatar or you can look up Commands once again.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Join Heaven's Gate (support server) and head to the corresponding section.
